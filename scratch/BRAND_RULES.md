### PINKOWL BRAND RULES (STRICT & MANDATORY)

**Colors Palette:**
Prefer these colors but gradients between non-brand colors are accepted.
- `hot_pink`: "#FF4081" (Primary accents, headlines, CTAs, energy)
- `owl_green`: "#0D7377" (Secondary accents, success, trust, grounding)
- `charcoal`: "#2D2D2D" (Body text only)
- `cream`: "#FFF8F0" (Primary background color)
- `light_green_bg`: "#E8F4F4" (Alternative background for "success/after" zones)
- `light_pink_bg`: "#FFE4EC" (Alternative background for "problem/before" zones)

**Owl Character:**
- Style: Geometric, cartoonish, clean lines, big expressive eyes.
- Rule: Must appear once. Body color must be EITHER `hot_pink` OR `owl_green`.
- Accent: The heart on its chest must be the opposite color of its body.
